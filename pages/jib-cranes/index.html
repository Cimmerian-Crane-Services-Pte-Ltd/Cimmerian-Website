Jib Crane

Compact rotating-arm crane designed for precision lifting in confined spaces — pillar-mounted or wall-mounted to suit your facility.

Rotating Arm Design

Compact Lifting
for Confined Spaces

The ABUS jib crane is specifically designed for applications where space is limited and precision lifting is required. Its pivoting arm provides excellent coverage of the work area without requiring the operator to move.

Available in both pillar-mounted and wall-mounted configurations, jib cranes offer a cost-effective solution for lifting tasks in workshops, manufacturing cells, and maintenance stations.

Features & Benefits

  • Increased mobility due to 270°-360° area of rotation
  • Load capacity up to 6.3 tons SWL (Safe Working Load)
  • Lightweight and compact design for easy installation
  • Available in pillar-mounted and wall-mounted types
  • Perfect for maintenance areas, production cells, and workshops

Configurations

Two Mounting Options,
One Reliable Brand

Pillar-Mounted

Self-supporting on a vertical pillar fixed to a concrete base. Ideal when no wall support is available.

  • Freestanding — no structural wall needed
  • 360° rotation for full coverage
  • Up to 6.3 tons SWL

Wall-Mounted

Attached to an existing wall or structure, saving valuable floor space. Perfect for production lines.

  • Zero floor footprint
  • 270° rotation along the wall face
  • Quick installation on solid structures
