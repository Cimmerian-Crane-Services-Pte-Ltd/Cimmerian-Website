Jib Crane

Jib cranes are ideal for lifting and handling objects in small or tight areas, offering increased mobility through their rotational design.

Rotating Arm Design

Compact Lifting
for Confined Spaces

The ABUS jib crane is specifically designed for applications where space is limited and precision lifting is required. Its pivoting arm provides excellent coverage of the work area without requiring the operator to move.

Available in both pillar-mounted and wall-mounted configurations, jib cranes offer a cost-effective solution for lifting tasks in workshops, manufacturing cells, and maintenance stations.

Pillar-Mounted

Self-supporting on a vertical pillar fixed to a concrete base. Ideal when no wall support is available.

  • Freestanding — no structural wall needed
  • 360° rotation for full coverage

Wall-Mounted

  • Zero floor footprint
  • 270° rotation along the wall face
