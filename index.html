Our History

1989

Cimmerian Crane was established in Singapore, beginning our journey as an overhead crane services provider.

1994

We broadened our range of operations, beginning sales and servicing of customers in Brunei, Saipan and many islands in the Indonesian Waters.

1996

We launched our 3-hour maximum response time for ad-hoc break-down calls upon activation — a feat unchallenged by competitors to this day.

2012

Became the sole distributor of ABUS Crane Systems in Singapore.
